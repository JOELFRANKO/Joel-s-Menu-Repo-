About JC

Joel's Cafe was found in 2024 September. Our Cafe is a small but family-friendly cafe located in the heart of Chennai City. We serve delicious and freshly baked goods, and we're always looking for new and exciting dessert ideas to try. Our Cafe was designed on the theame of Cold Forest and Sprakling rain. We hope you'll join us for a delightful evening and enjoy our warm coffee with a cold rainy dense atmosphere.☕☕☕

Cafee Trivia

where did the word cafe come from?

The English term café, borrowed from the French, derives ultimately from the Turkish kahve, meaning coffee.

was the first cafe opened?

The first café is said to have opened in 1550 in Constantinople; during the 17th century cafés opened in Italy, France, Germany, and England.
